Shelter Service

Service Name: Safe Haven

Safe Haven is a shelter service designed for individuals and families that need temporary housing. We provide warm meals, comfortable sleeping facilities, and comprehensive support services to help our guests find their footing and transition into stable housing. Our team of professional social workers and volunteers are here to assist in any way possible.
